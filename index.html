What this dashboard tells you

For job seekers

Use the search box to check whether a skill is being asked for more often now or less often than before.

For career changers

Look for skills that are stable or rising so you can focus your learning time where demand is stronger.

For hiring teams

Compare skill names and spot shifts in the language used in job posts before they become obvious.
